          Frequently Asked Questions

          PrintXpand is a complete web-to-print platform for commercial printers, retail decorators, promotional product distributors, print-on-demand businesses, and B2B print suppliers. It's a connected suite: eCommerce store, AI design tool, 3D configurator, print MIS/ERP, and connected seller platforms — Managed Cloud or On-Premise.
          PrintXpand offers PX Cloud and PX Cloud+ (Managed Cloud subscription) and PX On-Premise (one-time license with full source code access, self-hosted). That's a good fit if you need data sovereignty or deep custom integrations.
          PrintXpand delivers a working proof-of-concept with your branding and products in days, not months. Full deployment timelines depend on customization scope, integrations, and modules selected.
          PrintXpand integrates natively with Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento, BigCommerce, Odoo, and Wix. There's also a headless REST API if you're running a proprietary platform or need a custom integration.
          PrintXpand Connect is PrintXpand's vendor supply chain network — a single API to SanMar, S&S Activewear, MidOcean, PF Concept and more — so you can sync inventory in real time and onboard 12+ vendors with pre-built connections without re-platforming.
          PrintXpand offers a Free POC — a fully working build with your branding, products, and workflows — so you can see the platform before committing. Contact the team to scope and schedule yours.
